Welcome to the Draw Mix Paint Online Course! What you will learn here is strictly a method for learning how to paint realism in oil. The method will teach you how to draw in proportion, how to mix natural color, and how to paint realism as you see it. After you're comfortable with the method and have painted a few paintings using it, you can leave the method behind and develop your own way of doing things. While you're going through the course, if you ever have any questions you can get help on our free forum.
